Birth Certificate Application Guidelines

To successfully apply for your Birth Certificate, it is essential that all fields in the application form are filled out correctly. Please take your time to carefully enter your information:

Personal Information

Provide the full name of the individual whose birth certificate is being requested, their date of birth, sex, and place of birth. Ensure that the details match official records to avoid discrepancies.

Parent/Guardian Details

Enter the names of the individual's parents or guardians as they appear on official documents. Include their contact details for verification purposes, if required.

Supporting Documents

Attach a copy of any required documents, such as proof of identity, hospital records, or a previous birth certificate (if applicable). Ensure the documents are clear and legible.

Notification

Once your application is approved, you will receive a notification with further instructions. You may be requested to visit the Arada Kifleketema office to collect the Birth Certificate.
