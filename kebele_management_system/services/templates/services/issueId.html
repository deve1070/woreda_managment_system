Kebele ID Application Guidelines

To successfully apply for your Kebele ID, it is essential that all fields in the application form are filled out correctly. Please take your time to carefully enter your information:

Personal Information

Provide your full name, date of birth, sex, age, and contact details accurately. Ensure the spelling of your name matches your official documents.

Address Details

Enter your house number accurately. Any missing or incorrect information may cause delays in processing.

Supporting Documents

If the application requires document uploads, make sure the files are clear, properly scanned, and in the required format.

Notification

If your application is approved, you will receive a notification with further instructions. You will be requested to visit the Arada Kifleketema office to finalize the process and collect your Kebele ID.
