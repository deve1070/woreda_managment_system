Marriage Certificate Application Guidelines

To successfully apply for your Marriage Certificate, it is essential that all fields in the application form are filled out correctly. Please take your time to carefully enter your information:

Personal Information

Provide the full names of both individuals applying for the certificate, their date of birth, and contact details. Ensure the information matches your official documents.

Marriage Details

Enter the date and location of the marriage. Specify any additional details required, such as witnesses' names or officiant information, if applicable.

Notification

Once your application is approved, you will receive a notification with further instructions. You may be requested to visit the Arada Kifleketema office to collect the Marriage Certificate.
