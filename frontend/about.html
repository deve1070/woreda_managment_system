Who We Are?

The Kebele Management System is designed to streamline service delivery and enhance communication within Arada Kifleketema Wereda 6 Kebele. By leveraging technology, we aim to address the challenges faced by residents and service providers in accessing and managing essential services efficiently.

Our goal is to create a sustainable model that can serve as a benchmark for other kebeles, promoting improved governance and community support through digital innovation.

Key Objectives

  • Improved Service Access: Simplify processes like ID and birth certificate applications through an online platform.
  • Enhanced Communication: Deliver important notices and updates to residents efficiently.
  • Organized Data Management: Reduce paperwork and streamline operations across departments.
  • Community Feedback: Provide a platform for residents to share feedback and suggestions.

Who Benefits?

  • Service Providers: Reduce work duplication and enhance efficiency.
  • Community Residents: Save time and effort by accessing services online.
  • Kebele Management: Improve overall organization and communication.
  • Investors: Access comprehensive information about services without physical visits.

Our Services

  • Online Applications: Apply for IDs, birth certificates, and other essential services online.
  • Notices and Updates: Stay informed with timely news and updates from the kebele.
  • Feedback System: Share your thoughts and suggestions to improve services.
  • Data Management: Streamline and secure data across key departments.
