Supportive Letter Application Guidelines

To successfully apply for a Supportive Letter, it is essential to provide all required information accurately. The supportive letter is issued to individuals for specific purposes, such as proof of financial status, marital status, or other needs. Please follow the guidelines below:

Personal Information

Provide your full name, date of birth, sex, age, and contact details. Ensure the details match your official identification records. These details are necessary for identification and verification purposes.

Identification Requirements

A valid Kebele ID number is required to apply for a supportive letter. Please provide your Kebele ID number in the application form. Ensure the number is accurate and matches the one on your ID card.

Reason for Application

Clearly state the reason for requesting the supportive letter. This could include:

  • Proof of financial status for social support programs.
  • Confirmation of unmarried status for official purposes.
  • Other specific needs requiring official documentation.
Make sure to explain your reason in detail to ensure the document meets your requirements.

Supporting Documents

Attach any necessary supporting documents relevant to your application. Examples include:

  • Proof of income or financial status, if applicable.
  • Any additional documents required for your specific request.
Ensure the documents are clear, properly scanned, and meet the submission guidelines.

Notification

If your application is approved, you will receive a notification with further instructions. You may be requested to visit the Arada Kifleketema office to collect your Supportive Letter.
